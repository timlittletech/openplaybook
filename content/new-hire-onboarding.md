A great first month builds **confidence, context, and connection**. This playbook
covers what to do, when, and who owns each step.

## Before day one

| When | Who | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Offer accepted | HR | Send welcome email with start date logistics |
| 1 week prior | IT | Order hardware; create accounts |
| 1 week prior | Manager | Assign onboarding buddy; share team roster |

## Week 1 — Setup & context

- Manager meets the hire at start of day (in person or video)
- Hardware handoff; verify access to email, chat, calendar, repository
- 1:1 with manager — discuss the role and the [Weekly Team Meeting](#weekly-team-meeting) cadence

## Checklist

- [ ] Send welcome email with day-one logistics
- [ ] Order hardware and create accounts
- [ ] Assign an onboarding buddy
- [ ] Schedule week-1 manager 1:1s
- [ ] Run the 30-day calibration review
